My Challenge


Fasting is a form of intermittent fasting. It consists of a daily fast of at least 16 hours, preferably every day. The articles that inspired me to do this challenge are available here & here.
In anticipation of a few extra pounds because of the holidays, I decided to take this challenge to experience this lifestyle and its benefits and see if it's bullshit. So I'm going to do an 16-hour intermittent fast for 30 days and you will see below a daily commentary about this challenge.
