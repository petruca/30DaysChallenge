When to make this trip?

In order to enjoy the landscapes and remember the scenes from the movies, it is best to leave at the end of winter. So there will be snow when you visit Norway which will remind you of the famous assault on Hoth of the rebel base. The weather will be ideal also for the United States and Tunisia.

Recommended hotels

You may be wondering where you could sleep in each of the cities you visit?

- Let's start with Tunisia where we recommend three different hotels. The first one in Matmata is the Toujane Inn. It will be perfect because of attractive prices and you can discover Tunisian culture like never before. Alferdaous Inn in Tataouine, for the same reasons as before, is recommended too. However we do not necessarily have any hotel to recommend in Djerba but you will find your happiness for a small amount of money.
- About your trip to Norway you will stay whatever happens in the only hotel in the city of Finse but before that we propose you the "Thon Hotel Storo" in Oslo. This one is rather well located (a little expensive certainly) and even offers you in its restaurant dishes of organic seasons.
- About the United States, in the same way as Djerba we do not necessarily have any hotel to recommend in San Francisco. On the other hand, close to Redwood Park, 10km away (17min by car), there is the "Prairie Beach Home" which is a house for rent in a quiet place in the middle of nature perfect for breathing!

Traditional Dishes

During your trip you will have the opportunity to taste different traditional dishes.
- In Tunisia, we suggest you the famous couscous, the chorba (barley semolina soup) or the chakchouka (a kind of ratatouille).
- In Norway, there are some tasty dishes such as gravlax salmon, beef stew called "Kalops" or "Hasselback" potatoes.
- Concerning the United States, useless to tell you that you will enjoy the different burgers, bagels...

Here are some sentences/words that will probably be useful during your trip (except in Tunisia because of they also speak French).

Merci de votre accueil --> "Thank you for your hospitality" (english) ; "Tak for din velkomst" (danish)
Comment allez vous ? --> "How are you" (english) ; "Hvordan har du det?" (danish)
Je ne comprends pas --> "I don't understand" (english) ; "Jeg forstår ikke" (danish)
Où puis-je trouver...? --> "Where can I find...?" (english) ; "Hvor kan jeg finde ...?" (danish)
A droite, à gauche, tout droit --> "On the right, « on the left, straight ahead" (english) ; "Højre, venstre, lige" (danish)
