Administrative Procedures

Before leaving, some formalities must be carried out in order to guarantee you a good trip.

01 --> Prepare your identity papers

In the EU and in France's neighbours (Monaco, Switzerland, Andorra), your valid identity card is sufficient. To travel to other countries, you need a biometric passport.

02 --> Think about your driver's license (if you want to rent a car)

Before travelling, check with your doctor that all your vaccinations are up to date and, depending on your destination, if special precautions should be taken. Vaccination against yellow fever is required at the entry of some countries.

03 --> Make a demand to get ESTA (Electronic System for Travel Authorization)

This should be done online well before departure and especially before buying an airline ticket, because without ESTA USA, the borders of this country will be closed to you.

04 --> Get vaccinated

Before travelling, check with your doctor that all your vaccinations are up to date and, depending on your destination, if special precautions should be taken. Vaccination against yellow fever is required at the entry of some countries.
