Star Wars Trip


As you probably know, the last movie of the Star Wars saga about the Skywalker's family will be released soon. On this occasion we propose you a trip that will awaken the force in you ! Who among Star Wars fans has never dreamed of going to Tatooine, Endor or Hoth ? Actually, those mythical places that made you dream aren't in a galaxy far far away...

Let's travel !

You will visit the Tunisia, location of Tatooine in the saga which is the home planet of the Skylwalker's familly.

Go to Norway to visit the city of Finse where the assault of the empire on the rebel base was filmed in the fifth opus.

You'll visit the park of Redwood in which was shot the scenes on the famous planet Endor in the sixth film.
